About Us

I, Vanra Iaseduhbarp, created this juice bar after I looked at a store-bought juice's ingredients and found a lot of preservtives, colors, and chemicals. I vowed to make a company which only served fresh, healthy, and pure juice. So I founded Jumpin' Juice on May 9, 2018. I eventually started three locations around the center of the U.S. More than three years after Jumpin' Juice was founded, our three locations have served almost 100,000 drinks.
